About Me

I am a Sales Engineer turned stay-at-home dad turned Software Developer and recent graduate of the Microsoft .NET Accelerated Language Program at Eleven Fifty Academy in Carmel, IN. After graduating from Purdue with a BS in Computer Integrated Manufacturing Technology, (Now MFET), I started a career in sales of integrated conveyor systems. It was a small company so I had to wear many hats including: Sales, Engineering Design, Proposal Generation and Installation Manager.

As my wife's career accelerated, I found new places to work while we moved around the Midwest: Indianapolis - Chicago - Omaha, NE - Batesville, IN - Indianapolis. In Batesville, we decided I would become a stay-at-home dad and take care of our two daughters full time. During that change in responsibility, I also started my own clock repair business to service and repair mechanical clocks.

I have always had an interest in coding and am passionate about working with customers to offer creative ways to solve their problems or provide a product that will increase the efficiency of their organization. With this in mind, I decided it was the best vehicle for a successful rentry into the work force. Eleven Fifty Academy will provide the jump start I need to continue my professional career.

In my personal life, I am involved in competitive running, fitness, golf and being a volunteer parent for my children's schools.

JavaScript

Code from the DevAid Template designed by Xiaoying Riley that was used to design this page.


    jQuery(document).ready(function($) {

    /* ======= Scrollspy ======= */
    $('body').scrollspy({ target: '#header', offset: 400});
    
    /* ======= Fixed header when scrolled ======= */
    
    $(window).bind('scroll', function() {
         if ($(window).scrollTop() > 50) {
             $('#header').addClass('navbar-fixed-top');
         }
         else {
             $('#header').removeClass('navbar-fixed-top');
         }
    });
   
    /* ======= ScrollTo ======= */
    $('a.scrollto').on('click', function(e){
        
        //store hash
        var target = this.hash;
                
        e.preventDefault();
        
        $('body').scrollTo(target, 800, {offset: -70, 'axis':'y', easing:'easeOutQuad'});
        //Collapse mobile menu after clicking
        if ($('.navbar-collapse').hasClass('in')){
            $('.navbar-collapse').removeClass('in').addClass('collapse');
        }
        
    });

});
